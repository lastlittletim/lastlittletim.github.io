idkiguessimmakingagamein24hoursnow

An endless arcade shooter(?) with inconvienent controls. Players can only get around by pushing themselves around with their gun, a la Getting Over It with Bennett Foddy, or by using the recoil from firing it. Enemies come in waves, and it gets faster until you die or give up. Created in a day as a challenge.

Project_1

A platformer that relies heavily on momentum. Players are given a rocket they can point in any direction to gain speed, but has its effectivity greatly reduced when not touching a floor or wall or some sort.

New Unity Project

A twin stick shooter where the walls extend if a bullet hits one. These walls extend until they touch another wall, and anything unfortunate enough to be there when it happens will be destroyed. I came up with the idea when thinking about things the genre usually takes for granted, in this case being able to spray bullets indiscriminately. If players are not careful with their shots, they easily end up with no space to move around, or die by getting crushed by a wall. Levels are proceedually generated, using a modified version of the code i wrote for AAAAAA
