IHNAMR

I Have No Artist and i Must RPG

What is this

IHNAMR is my very own turn based rpg engine that combines traditional JRPG action with elements of turn-based strategy games like party placement. Built from the ground up (well as ground up as unity allows) and held together with spaghetti and duct tape, everything in IHNAMR was made by me over the course of the last 4 months.

Enemy recruitment

Player units and enemies share a class, allowing you to recruit enemies for your party on the fly.

Menu Designs

In case you couldn't tell from how bad the website looks, I'm not the biggest designer out there. So, designing the UI for this was a bit of a pain, but I'm satisfied with how it turned out.

Proceedual Generation

Dungeons are proceedually generated, with easy to adjust options that allow for anything from short paths to impractically complex labyrinths, and a ability to choose where on the scale you want the generated levels to be
