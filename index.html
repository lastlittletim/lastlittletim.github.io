About me

I've been making games since I first got my start on Scratch back in 2015, where I made incredibly ok at best games which you can check out here. I've spent most of my career on a Raspberry Pi. I mainly work with C# and Unity(2D), but I can also do Unity(3D), python, a bit of javascript, and now html.

While I've mostly tried to learn by myself on the internet, I've also had the honor of training under Chaoclypse, a solo game developer, artist and all around cool guy.

My art skills are questionable at best, so expect to see a lot of particle effects and basic geometry in my work
