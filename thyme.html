IHNAMR

I Have No Artist and i Must RPG

What is this

IHNAMR is my turn based rpg engine that combines traditional JRPG action with elements of turn-based strategy games like party placement. Built from the ground up (well as ground up as unity allows) and held together with spaghetti and duct tape, everything in IHNAMR was made by me over the course of the last 4 months.

Enemy recruitment

Player units and enemies share a class, allowing you to recruit enemies for your party on the fly.

Battle menu

I'm not the best on the UI/UX side of development, but I've done my best to make a menu that takes as little time as possible to get where you want to, as that's normally the biggest timesink in an RPG

Proceedual Generation

Dungeons are proceedually generated, with easy to adjust options that allow for anything from short paths to impractically complex labyrinths, and a ability to choose where on the scale you want the generated levels to be
